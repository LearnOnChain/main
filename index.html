LearnOnChain

Our Vision - An Educational Web3 Platform secured and powered on the blockchain where anyone can learn and educate in a fully decentralised environment

Our Mission - Revolutionise the current education system by providing a secure, transparent and personalised digital learning experience

Our Goal - Provide the opportunities to learn and educate an endless range of subjects taught in virtual classrooms, lecture theatres, or via 1:1 tutoring

Overview

The current education system is outdated. It is expensive, inefficient, and inaccessible to many people.

It is also vulnerable to cyberattacks with data management and subsequent security an issues

The global education market is worth an estimated $5.7 trillion with a rapidly accelerating shift to online learning.

Countries which have the highest cryptocurrency ownership rate also rank poorly in terms of accessibility to education

Our platform will use blockchain technology to create a more secure, transparent, and affordable modern education system.

The decentralized environment will foster a global ecosystem of students and teachers to connect

We believe that our platform has the potential to revolutionize the education system to a more accessible and affordable model that doesn't discriminate against a student's background, culture, or location

Platform Overview

Decentralized Learning Environment

Students and teachers create and own their data in a global, digitalized platform. Users participate in platform development decisions, voting on proposals and DAO spending

Governance & Security

All users on the platform are vetted with an overarching governance model regulating their privacy and Personal Identifiable Information (PII). The LearnOnChain Master Users will authorize changes, remove users, and ensure high-level teaching standards

Digital Credentials

Students will store their education history, learning, and development journey on the blockchain. This provides a secure and transparent platform to track their progress and reliably showcase their achievements

Personalized Learning

Leveraging the Blockchain, LearnOnChain calculates each student's learning style, preferences, and interests to provide a customized learning experience.

Microlearning

LearnOnChain provides bite-sized, easy-to-digest learning modules, enhancing student engagement and knowledge retention. Students can learn at their own pace and receive continuous feedback, ensuring they are on the right track.

Macrolearning

Alternatively, students can enrol in long-term courses over several months with limitless opportunities for exploring subjects and topics.

Engaging

Students can explore the LearnOnChain World with NFT Avatars representing their character. Outside of a classroom environment, users can enjoy educational games, in-platform communication with other students, and social networking.

NFT Marketplace

Users can access thousands of items to enhance their avatars and study tools, alongside owning building assets for L2E benefits.

Learn 2 Earn (L2E)

In-platform tokens can be earnt through participating in learning, attending classes, and owning educational buildings.

Tokenomics

$LEARN - A Non-Inflationary Token used for in-platform education & teaching financials, plus voting and DAO Governance

  • Students – Use $LEARN to enrol in classes, attend lectures & book private tutoring sessions
  • Teachers – Will earn $LEARN
  • NFTs - The main in-platform Educational buildings (Classrooms, lecture theatres, and tutoring rooms) can be acquired by using $LEARN
  • NFT holders act as landlords and earn $LEARN when learning takes place inside their buildings
